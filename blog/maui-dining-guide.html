Maui Kai 806 Dining Guide

Your friendly host's curated list of can't-miss eateries—organized by region

January 2025
Dining Guide

After years of exploring Maui's culinary scene, we've compiled our personal favorites to help you discover the island's best flavors. From casual food trucks to fine dining experiences, here's where we love to eat—and where we send our guests for memorable meals.

Ultimate Favorite: TinRoof

Kahului – Near Airport & Costco

📍 360 Papa Pl Ste 116, Kahului, HI 96732

📞 (808) 868-0753 • 🌐 tinroofmaui.com

Chef Sheldon Simeon of Top Chef fame brings nostalgic Hawaiian plate lunches to the next level with mochiko chicken, garlic shrimp, Fat Chow Fun, and fish sandwiches.

My Personal Favorites:

  • Mochiko Chicken
  • Garlic Shrimp
  • Garlic Noodles
  • Pork Belly
  • Fish Sando

Pro tip: Pre-order online, pick up, and enjoy in your car—perfect arrival or departure fuel.

West Maui (Lahaina, Kaanapali, Kapalua)

🏝️ Notable Spots Near Maui Kai 806

These restaurants are within walking distance of the condo and are popular with many visitors:

🏖️

Duke's Beach House

Oceanfront dining with tropical cocktails and live music. Great for a convenient, scenic meal.

🚚

Honokowai Food Truck Park

A rotating mix of casual food trucks just north of Maui Kai. Good for a quick bite, though options can vary.

🛍️

Whalers Village

A 15–20 minute walk or short drive south along the Kaanapali Beach Walk, featuring restaurants like Hula Grill, Leilani's on the Beach, and Monkeypod Kitchen.

Note: Merriman's Kapalua is often mentioned in Maui guides, but it is a 15–20 minute drive north from Maui Kai—not walkable.

🍻 Maui Brewing Co. (Lahaina/Kaanapali)

🍺

Maui Brewing Co.

📍 Kaanapali Beach Hotel, Lahaina

Smaller brewpub location with ocean-adjacent seating and a local beer lineup. Menu is more limited compared to Kihei flagship, but still a solid spot for craft brews near the beach.

🍜 Star Noodle

🍜

Star Noodle

📍 1287 Front St, Lahaina

starnoodle.com

Oceanfront favorite and popular spot for modern ramen and garlic noodles with creative cocktails. Great place for sunset dinner—reservations strongly recommended in advance.

🍽️ Aloha Mixed Plate

🍽️

Aloha Mixed Plate

📍 1285 Front St, Lahaina

alohamixedplate.com

Hawaiian plate classics—kalbi ribs and ocean-view outdoor seating at sunset.

🍣 Sansei Seafood & Sushi Bar

🍣

Sansei Seafood & Sushi Bar

📍 600 Office Rd, Kapalua

sanseihawaii.com

Fresh, highly praised sushi. Grab the 50% off early bird special Sunday & Monday 5–5:45 PM.

🍣 Miso Phat Sushi

🍣

Miso Phat Sushi

📍 4310 Lower Honoapiilani Rd #111, Lahaina

misophatlahaina.com

Creative, casual sushi spot; BYOB makes it feel relaxed and fun.

🥂 Fond

🥂

Fond

📍 5095 Napilihau St #115, Lahaina

fondmaui.com

Cozy neighborhood gem with thoughtful fusion dishes and attentive service.

🍗 L&L Hawaiian BBQ (Lahaina)

🍗

L&L Hawaiian BBQ

📍 3350 Lower Honoapiilani Rd #4

llhawaiianbarbecuelahaina.com

Local-style plate lunches, consistently praised for quality.

South Maui (Wailea & Kihei)

🍣 Oao Sushi

🍣

Oao Sushi

📍 34 Wailea Gateway Pl, Kihei

oaowailea.com

Excellent sushi—highlights include Karaage Salmon. Quality worth the splurge. Happy Hour 3-5 PM daily.

🧲 Island Gourmet Market

🧲

Island Gourmet Market

📍 3750 Wailea Alanui Dr, Wailea

(808) 874-5055 islandgourmethawaii.com

A lifestyle gourmet market offering fresh produce, poke bowls, hot and cold deli meals, fresh-baked goods, coffee bar, beer & wine, and sundries. Great for grab-and-go.

🍧 Ululani's Shave Ice

🍧

Ululani's Shave Ice

📍 61 S Kihei Rd, Kihei

ululanishawaiianshaveice.com

Considered Maui's best shave ice—long lines, but totally worth it.

🍹 Lineage

🍹

Lineage

📍 The Shops at Wailea

lineagemaui.com

Hawaiian-fusion comfort food with ocean views—crowd favorites: Korean fried chicken and lobster noodles.

☕ Akamai Coffee

☕

Akamai Coffee

📍 116 Wailea Ike Dr, Kihei (+ Kahului)

akamaicoffee.com

Locally grown Maui beans—Maui Mokka is a must-try.

🍱 Da Kitchen

🍱

Da Kitchen

📍 1215 S Kihei Rd, Kihei

dakitchen.com

Hawaiian plate-lunch classics—Chicken Katsu is a highlight.

🍻 Maui Brewing Co. (Kihei - Flagship Location)

🍻

Maui Brewing Co.

📍 605 Lipoa Pkwy, Kihei

mbcrestaurants.com

Flagship brewery with spacious taproom, local craft beers, full food menu, and live music. Great for groups.

Note: Maui Brewing also has a Lahaina/Kaanapali brewpub with a more limited menu.

🍞 Kihei Caffe

🍞

Kihei Caffe

📍 1945 S Kihei Rd, Kihei

kiheicaffe.com

Classic breakfast and brunch meals that locals love.

🍾 Nalu's South Shore Grill

🍾

Nalu's South Shore Grill

📍 1280 S Kihei Rd, Kihei

naluskihei.com

Dependable breakfast fare with island-style flair.

🍝 Monkeypod Kitchen by Merriman

🍝

Monkeypod Kitchen by Merriman

📍 Wailea Gateway Pl

monkeypodkitchen.com

Great atmosphere, tasty cocktails, and a popular happy hour scene.

Between Lahaina & Kihei (Olowalu / Maalaea)

🍽️ Haven's Harborside

🍽️

Haven's Harborside

📍 300 Maalaea Rd, Wailuku

havensharborside.com

Waterside happy hour—award-winning chef in a scenic harbor setting.

🥧 Leoda's Kitchen & Pie Shop

🥧

Leoda's Kitchen & Pie Shop

📍 820 Olowalu Village Rd

leodas.com

Casual dining with freshly baked bread and legendary pies. Must-tries: banana cream, coconut cream, chocolate mac nut. Pro tip: Bring an insulated bag to take extra pies home if you're traveling around the island!

Paia / Road to Hana Region

🐟 Mama's Fish House

🐟

Mama's Fish House

📍 799 Poho Pl, Paia

mamasfishhouse.com

Legendary beachfront seafood destination—a special-occasion favorite with unmatched ocean views.

Markets & Quick Stops

🌟 Foodland (Multiple Locations)

🌟

Foodland

Local favorite for fresh poke bowls and Spam musubi. Convenient grab-and-go options great for a beach day or road trip fuel. Visit the Kahului, Lahaina, or Kihei locations for consistency and fast service.

🧲 Island Gourmet Market (Wailea)

🧲

Island Gourmet Market

📍 3750 Wailea Alanui Dr, Wailea

(808) 874-5055 islandgourmethawaii.com

Lifestyle market with fresh produce, hot & cold deli items, sushi, baked goods, wine/beer, and beach gear. Ideal for casual takeout or a quick, quality meal without the sit-down commitment.

Still Want to Try

🥐

Shikeda Bento Patisserie (Wailuku)

Japanese-French pastries & bentos; Tues–Sat midday hours

🥗

Spoon & Key (Wailea)

Clean, healthy bowls and modern fare

🍖

Only Ono BBQ (Paia)

New place to try! Standout items include Siu Mai, Chicken Curry Puffs, and roast pork. Don't miss the Bolo Bao and Dan Tats. If Texas Brisket is available, order it!

Bon Appétit!

This guide represents our personal favorites after years of exploring Maui's culinary scene. Whether you're looking for a quick bite near the condo or planning a special dinner, these spots have never disappointed us or our guests.

Remember to make reservations for popular spots, especially during peak season. And don't forget to try the local specialties—poke bowls, shave ice, and Hawaiian plate lunches are must-tries!
