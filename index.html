Fully Equipped Kitchen

Complete kitchen with fridge, stove, microwave, dishwasher, drip coffee maker, blender and toaster. Includes all pots, pans, cutlery, cups and plates. Starter supplies: paper towel, dish soap, dishwasher tablets, cook top spray, salt and pepper.

Climate Control

Central air conditioning controlled from our unit, completely updated with new thermostat. High output ceiling fan for better air circulation when you don't want to use AC.

High-Speed Internet

FREE unlimited in-room high-speed WiFi for your computer and wireless devices. Private password provided for secure connection.

Entertainment

55 inch Samsung LED Smart TV with Spectrum HD digital cable and On Demand viewing. Binoculars provided for ocean viewing.

Resort Amenities

Heated swimming pool and Jacuzzi spa/hot tub. Poolside cabana with full kitchen and lending library. Cabana computer with printer for complimentary access.

BBQ & Laundry

Poolside gas barbecue grills available. Coin operated washers and dryers on almost every floor. Plenty of FREE parking included.

Beach Access & Gear

Close access to two miles of walking beach and boardwalk. In-room beach towels provided. Beach chairs, beach umbrella, boogie board, cooler in lanai storage bin.

Bathroom & Linens

Custom tiled bathroom with standing shower. Hair dryer and starter soap, shampoo, conditioner and body lotion provided. Full cleaning service at check out. Towels, linens and supplies at check in.

Sleeping & Comfort

California KING sized bed with hotel quality Beautyrest mattress. Sofa love seat. Iron, ironing board, and laundry basket provided.

Lanai & Views

Large west-facing private lanai with new easy slide thermo cool balcony doors and new bar high chairs. Unobstructed panoramic ocean views to Lanai and Molokai.

Location & Access

Conveniently located on north section of Kaanapali Beach near Black Rock. Two high-speed elevators to 8th floor. Walking distance to Times Supermarket, restaurants, and shops.

Parking & Transportation

Plenty of FREE parking included. Short drive to world famous golf courses and hiking. Easy access to water sport rentals like Boss Frogs.

Maui Tips & Local Knowledge

Insider information from Jason and Gloria, your Maui Kai 806 hosts

Getting Here

Address: Maui Kai Oceanfront Condos, 106 Kaanapali Shores Place, Lahaina, Maui, HI 96761

Airport: Kahului Airport (OGG) - 45 minute drive

Car Rentals

We recommend Aloha Rent A Car for great rates on older but reliable vehicles. Also try Manaloha or Kimo's for budget options.

Major companies available at OGG airport as well.

Shopping & Supplies

Nearby: Times Supermarket (5 min walk), Safeway in Lahaina

Airport: Costco & Walmart for initial supplies

Sunday Market: Maui Gift and Craft Fair at Lahaina Gateway

Dining Nearby

Local Favorites: Ono Kau Kau Mixed Plate, Pizza Paradiso (5 min walk)

Beachfront: Duke's Beach House (short walk through park)

Fine Dining: Mala, Honu Seafood in Lahaina

Water Activities

Whale Watching: Ultimate Whale Watch, Pacific Whale Foundation (Nov-May)

Snorkeling: Molokini Crater tours, Black Rock, Turtle Town

Sunset Sails: SailingMaui.com, Trilogy Ocean Adventures

Adventures

Hiking: Kapalua trails, Iao Valley, Haleakala National Park

ATV Tours: Kahoma Ranch ATV and Waterslides

Kayaking: Kayak Olowalu guided tours
